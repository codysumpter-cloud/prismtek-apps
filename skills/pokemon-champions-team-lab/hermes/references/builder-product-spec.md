# Builder Product Spec Notes

## Product shape
Describe the product as a **best-fit team builder**.

## Required user inputs
- format
- regulation snapshot
- locked Pokémon: 0–6
- style preference
- risk tolerance
- goal

## Required outputs
- one best team or top candidates
- full sets
- replacements for weak or illegal slots
- strategy plan
- matchup warnings
- export surface

## Deterministic engine owns
- legality
- regulation snapshot
- candidate generation
- scoring
- replacement logic

## AI owns
- explanations
- strategy summaries
- naming
- clearer wording
