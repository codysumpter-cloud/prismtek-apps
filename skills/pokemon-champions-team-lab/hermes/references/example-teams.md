# Example Team Shapes

## Stable Singles
- pivot lead
- progress maker
- physical breaker
- special breaker
- speed control
- endgame cleaner

## Stable Doubles
- coherent lead
- speed control or Trick Room mode
- support slot
- spread pressure
- immediate breaker
- cleaner or bulky endgame

## Identity rule
Preserve the team’s weird plan when it can be made legal and coherent.
Replace only the slots that are illegal, unsupported, or structurally dead.
